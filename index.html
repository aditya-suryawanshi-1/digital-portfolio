PERFORMANCE AND EMISSION ANALYSIS OF A DIESEL ENGINE UNDER VARYING JATROPHA, ETHANOL,AND DIETHYL ETHER BLEND

This study will determine, through efficiency, power, fuel consumption, and emission for various blends, theoptimal blend that realizes both environmental and performance improvements.
